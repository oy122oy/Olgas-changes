Software Developper
We’re looking for a software developer. Not a “rockstar ninja guru”. Just… someone who can code without setting things on fire. You can work from home. Wear shorts. Wear flip flops. Wear a hoodie that hasn’t seen daylight since 2020. We don’t care. No need to sound fancy in meetings either — if you can explain your code without using words like “synergy”, you’re already ahead. Your main job? Sit comfortably. Write code. Fight bugs. Repeat. You can work from your sofa, your desk, your bed… honestly, if inspiration strikes while you’re in the bathroom, we’re not asking questions. If you can build things, fix things, and occasionally defeat a bug that’s been ruining everyone’s day — we want you. Apply now. Or later. We know you’re busy debugging.

Back End Developer
Yes, We Know You’re Back There Somewhere. We’re looking for a backend developer. We know you exist… you’re just usually hiding behind APIs and databases where no one can see you. We offer good working conditions — mainly meaning you can stay in your natural habitat (your “nest”), keep your questionable habits, and focus on building things that actually work. All we ask is this: remind us you’re alive once in a while. Something like: “Hey boss, database is ready. Anything else?” That’s it. Communication level: minimal but reassuring. Show up to a meeting once a month, keep the backend running, and honestly… you’re a hero to us. If that sounds like your vibe, come join us. We promise not to drag you into unnecessary front-end drama.

Boss
With a boss, we are four productive, semi-responsible humans. Without a boss… we are four people watching dog videos and calling it “research.”
Our previous one tried. They really did. But somewhere between “quick stand-up meeting” and “why is nothing done?”, they slowly lost the will to continue. We wish them well.
