How To Find Us

destination = f(you)

🚌

By Bus

Routes 1, 4, and 33 stop directly on Bishop's Rd. Alight at Queensgate Bus Station (5 min walk). Frequency ≈ every 12 minutes during peak hours. Bus(arrival) → Walk(0.4km) = You Are Here ✓

🚆

By Train

Alight at Peterborough Station — 15 min walk or a 4 min taxi. Trains run from London King's Cross (50 min), Birmingham (1hr 20). Train(t) + Walk(900m) = Math0ryx. Simple.

🚗

By Car

From the A1(M) take junction 17 → follow signs to City Centre → Bishop's Rd. Nearest car park: Queensgate NCP (3 min walk). Parking rate: £2.50/hr — cheaper than failing your maths exam.

On Foot

From city centre: head north along Bridge St → left onto Bishop's Rd → University House is on your right. d = 0.6km, t ≈ 8 minutes at average walking speed. You've got this.
